COVID-19 Ventilator

Physical proof-of-concept

In MSE312 (Mechatronics Design II), we were given the option to choose our final project. Our team of 3 students chose to design a COVID-19 ventilator which used a proportional solenoid valve (PSV) to precisely meter the air flow directed to a patient.

    List of primary components:
  • Honeywell Flow Sensor HAFUHT0200L4AXT
  • SMC PSV PVQ31-6G-23-01N
  • Arduino Mega 2560
  • SMC Air Regulator, Series AR30-B, 1/4 NPT Female

    • Desired flow rate from ~1 (l/min) to ~36 (l/min)


    This project led to skills development in feedback control, fluid dynamics simulations, hardware assembly, and MCU (Arduino) programming.
